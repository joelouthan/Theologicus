# Praying Through: Psalm 119:16

## The Psalm

>Psalms 119:16 (CSB) I will delight in your statutes; I will not forget your word.

## The Prayer

<div style="font-variant: small-caps;">
My Living Word
</div>

Every word
* from your mouth
* has resurrected my soul
* from death
* to life.

For that
* I will praise your holy name.

Amen.
