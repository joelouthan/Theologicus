# Praying Through: Psalm 119:23

## The Psalm

>Psalm 119:23 Though princes sit together speaking against me, your servant will think about your statutes;

## The Prayer

<div style="font-variant: small-caps;">
My Living Word
</div>

There is nothing on this earth
* that can keep me away
* nor separate me
* from your precious love.

I know this
* because I have your Son
* my precious, lovely
* Lord Jesus Christ.

Amen.
